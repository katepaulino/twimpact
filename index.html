TwImpact: Making an impact via Twitter

This page allows you to tweet your congressional leader about key bills the Borgen Project is currently working on.

Instructions:

  • Search your representative by entering your address
  • Next, click on the issue you would like to tweet your representative about
  • As soon as you see the auto message appear in the tweet box, send the tweet!
